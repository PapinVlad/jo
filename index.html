About me

Hello everyone! My name is Jo Smith, and I’m a passionate photographer based in Dundee. I’ve recently graduated with a degree in Photography, and I’m thrilled to start capturing beautiful moments that mean the world to you! Photography is not just my career; it’s my way of expressing creativity and connecting with people on a deeper level. I love bringing unique ideas to life, both yours and mine, and creating timeless memories for you to cherish.

My approach is flexible and client-focused. If you need help deciding on outfits, themes, or anything related to the photoshoot, I’ll be there to guide you every step of the way. You can send me photos of your wardrobe, and together, we’ll pick the perfect look. During the shoot, I’ll assist with posing, finding the most flattering angles, and making sure everyone feels comfortable. After all, the best photos come from genuine, happy moments that tell your story.

Looking forward to working with you and capturing your most joyful, vibrant, and heartfelt memories!

Portrait photo session – shooting of one person by a photographer. It can be a business portrait, street style, lifestyle, a walk around the city, in nature, studio or boudoir shooting.

Such a photo shoot is necessary, first of all, to please yourself. And secondly, for work, portfolio, resume, Instagram and other social networks.

Every couple has their own romantic, special love story. This kind of shooting will help you convey your emotions and capture feelings.

This type of photo shoot can be done for a special occasion, for example, before a wedding or on each of your anniversaries, but sometimes you don't need a reason at all to save a moment. After all, love has no boundaries, and it gets stronger every year!

This type of shooting is suitable for couples with children. This way you can capture the brightest moments of your happy family life.

Photography will give you the opportunity to see how your children change and grow. Only in this way will you be able to preserve for many years the memories of your baby's first birthday, his first successes and happy changes in your family.

Pregnancy is a special and exciting period in the life of every woman and her family. That's why it's worth capturing!

The ideal time for such a touching and tender photo shoot is the seventh or eighth month of pregnancy. The photo session can be held in any interior photo studio or outdoors.

Pets fill our lives with joy and give us boundless love. That's why many people want to look at beautiful and funny photos of their pet.

This type of shooting is most often suitable for nurseries that breed and sell animals, as well as people who want to capture bright moments with their favorite pets. Such shooting can take place in a photo studio or outdoors.

Weddings are one of the most significant milestones in our lives, filled with love, joy, and unforgettable moments. This special day deserves to be captured in all its beauty to preserve the memories that you will cherish forever.

To ensure your wedding memories are timeless and heartfelt, you need stunning photographs that reflect the essence of your unique love story. I will work closely with you to capture every precious moment, from the intimate preparations to the grand celebrations, creating images that you and your loved ones will treasure for a lifetime.
